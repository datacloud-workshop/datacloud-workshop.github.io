Salesforce Data Cloud Use Cases

The following are use cases which are generally applicable across all industries. You could use these as a starting point to further identify business use cases for your industry/company. For example, for financial services some use cases could be: Abandoned Application Loan Applications, New Account Onboarding, Customer Acquisition, Product Cross-sell, Compute Financial Health Score, Investment Advice.

Sales


Referal sharing across multiple orgs. Orchestrate leads across all business units.

Personalize customer interactions with Customer360 based on unified profiles.

Identify product cross-sell opportunities with unified enterprise data.

Marketing, Analytics


Precise segmentation by combining transactional CRM data with web/mobile engagement behavioral data.

Real time analytics with accelerated queries.

Accurate AI predictions & scores with aggregated date & BYOM

Service


Multi-BU cases visibility.

Personalized Next Best Action based on CRM + engagement data.
